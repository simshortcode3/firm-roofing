When to Repair vs. Replace Your Roof

Kenya’s Expert Guide to Making the Right Roofing Decision

Know the Right Time for Action

Your roof protects your entire home from Kenya’s changing climate—from torrential rains to extreme heat. But how do you know whether to repair or replace it entirely? Here's what our experts say.

Repair If:

  • You have a few missing or cracked tiles
  • Leaks are isolated to specific areas
  • The roof is under 15 years old
  • Damage was caused by a recent storm

Replace If:

  • You notice sagging or structural weaknesses
  • There are repeated leaks despite repairs
  • The roof is older than 20–25 years
  • Repair costs exceed 30% of replacement cost

Repair vs Replace: Quick Comparison

Roof Repair

✅ Faster
✅ Cost-effective
✅ Great for isolated issues

Roof Replacement

✅ Long-term durability
✅ Energy-efficient
✅ Increases property value

Real Case: Nairobi Apartment Block

"Initially, we kept patching the leaks. After 3 years, we replaced the roof entirely. It ended up saving us thousands in maintenance." — Facilities Manager, Nairobi

Expert Tips

  • Always get a roof inspection before deciding
  • Request a quote for both repair and replacement
  • Factor in your future plans—sell or stay?
