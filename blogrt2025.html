Why 2025 Is a Big Year for Commercial Roofing

Kenyan businesses are rapidly upgrading their roofing systems to keep pace with sustainability goals, energy efficiency, and cost-saving innovations. Here’s what to expect in 2025—and how to stay ahead.

1. Solar-Integrated Roofs

More businesses are installing solar tiles or panels to cut power costs and meet green energy targets.

2. Energy-Efficient Cool Roofs

Reflective membranes are reducing cooling bills by up to 20%, especially in Nairobi and Mombasa offices.

3. Sustainable Roofing Materials

Expect demand for recycled metal and TPO membranes to grow as environmental regulations tighten.

4. Impact-Resistant Roofs

Businesses are investing in long-term safety with hail- and storm-resistant surfaces for warehouses and malls.
