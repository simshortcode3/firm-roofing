Choosing the Perfect Roof for Kenya's Diverse Climate

Your Guide to Climate-Smart Roofing Solutions

Why Climate Matters in Roof Selection

Kenya's unique climate zones demand specialized roofing solutions. From the humid coastal regions to the arid northern territories and the rainy highlands, your roof needs to withstand:

Coastal Challenges

  • High humidity (up to 80%)
  • Salt spray corrosion
  • Heavy monsoon rains

Arid Areas

  • Extreme heat (up to 40°C)
  • UV radiation
  • Dust storms

Highland Needs

  • Heavy rainfall (2000mm+ annually)
  • Hailstorms
  • Cooler temperatures

Top 5 Roofing Materials for Kenyan Homes

1. Galvanized Steel

Best for: Coastal & Highland regions
Benefits: Corrosion-resistant, 30-year lifespan
Cost: KES 1,500 - 2,500 per m²

2. Clay Tiles

Best for: Arid & Urban areas
Benefits: Natural insulation, fireproof
Cost: KES 2,000 - 3,500 per m²

Pro Tips from Kenyan Roofers

  • 30° roof pitch ideal for heavy rainfall areas
  • Use sisal insulation for natural cooling
  • Install gutter guards during long rains
