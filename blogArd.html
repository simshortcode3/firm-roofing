Why Architectural Roofing Matters

In Kenya’s ever-evolving housing market, roof design is no longer just functional—it's a statement. Whether you're building in Nairobi, Kisumu, or the coast, the right architectural roof boosts curb appeal, energy efficiency, and even resale value.

1. Gable Roofs

Benefits: Timeless aesthetic, excellent water drainage
Best for: Rainy highland regions

2. Hip Roofs

Benefits: Wind-resistant, modern look
Best for: Coastal and windy areas

3. Flat Roofs

Benefits: Easy to install solar panels and rooftop gardens
Best for: Urban developments

4. Curved Roofs

Benefits: Stylish and futuristic design
Best for: High-end or custom homes
