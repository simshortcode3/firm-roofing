Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our roofing services and solutions.

We typically recommend either metal roofing or concrete tiles for most Kenyan homes. Metal roofs are lightweight, durable, and excellent for water runoff, while concrete tiles offer excellent insulation and a more traditional look. The best choice depends on your budget, climate, and aesthetic preferences.

The duration depends on the size and complexity of the project. A standard residential roof installation typically takes 2-4 weeks, while commercial projects may take 4-8 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Yes, we offer comprehensive warranties on both materials and workmanship. Our standard warranty covers materials for 10-20 years (depending on type) and workmanship for 5 years. Extended warranties are available for certain premium products.

We recommend professional roof inspections at least once every two years, and after any major weather event. Regular visual inspections by the homeowner should be done seasonally to check for any visible damage or wear.

We provide roofing services throughout Kenya, with our main operations in Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, and Eldoret. For projects outside these areas, additional transportation costs may apply.

Signs that your roof may need attention include: leaks or water stains, missing or damaged shingles/tiles, sagging areas, excessive granule loss (for asphalt roofs), or if your roof is over 20 years old. Our team can assess your roof and recommend the most cost-effective solution.
